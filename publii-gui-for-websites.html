The Quest for Deep Work

Adrian Ochoa

Having read Cal Newport's Deep Work, I desire to document my pursuit in creating a better work-life balance by training my mind to be disciplined in this art of focus and "deep work", a term that Newport contrasts with "shallow work". I will write about my journey living a deep and focused life, pursuing my main interests of mathematics, music, spoken language and general nerd potpourri.

My life in code:

while (alive):
    work_deeply()

While there are other things to do besides work, centering our lives around our quiet, distraction-free, focused work time is essential for a satisfying and quality life. We all have to make money to live and survive. We may as well do it in the most wholesome way we can: ensure that when you work, you are focused, undistracted and attaining depth. This will derive great satisfaction.

For me, deep work includes doing mathematics for my day job, mastering musical pieces that interest me and are at my skill level and studying foreign language to better communicate with my loved ones and for the upcoming unscheduled trip to Europe and South Asia one day (dream trips I hope to come true).
